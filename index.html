    Team AutoZ, is a research & development team driven by undergraduate students of VIT Vellore who are working on developing autonomous vehicles and automated systems. Our team comprises a diverse set of people who are having prior experience in industrial projects. We are making the best out of our resources with such an amazing and motivated team of young people. Our mission is to design and develop products in solving real world use cases, and to work with companies that are keen at trying to bring automation into the market.

    Goals

    We plan on taking part in Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition(IGVC)- IGVC is an international event under the basket of Autonomous technology. IGVC is one of the most prestigious automation technology event that takes place internationally every year and multiple teams try and build efficient autonomous bots and win the competition. We are going to be one among the 5-6 indian teams that participate in this international competition next year. We believe that IGVC, will shape our team and take us forward In our goal to play key roles in the development of efficient automation technology in the future.
